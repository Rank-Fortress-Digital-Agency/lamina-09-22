HOW TO BORROW

Read More

Complete the pre-approval process to determine your eligibility


You’ll get all the information you need to make an informed decision in 5 minutes no obligation to borrow.

Complete the loan application

This step also takes around 5 minutes. You’ll select the amount you’d like to borrow and provide some personal information needed in the loan contract.

Visit Us

Sign the contract


You’ll be able to read it and sign it electronically on your smartphone or PC.

Use the bank link


You’ll receive a link by text message and email that will allow you to return an up to date bank statement directly from your bank. It takes under 3 minutes and it streamlines the process so you don’t have to prepare and return any documents.
